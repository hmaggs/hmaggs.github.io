A little bit about myself...

My name is Hari, a nickname created by my parents when they realized that no one could pronounce my given Welsh name, Angharad. I grew up in San Diego, California and therefore have a never-dying passion for beaches, burritos, and being in the ocean. I have been lucky enough to be able to travel to the United Kingdom every year to visit my very large-in-numbers extended family, and during this time I try to visit other countries whenever I can. I spent a semester in Sydney, Australia during the Fall of 2014 where I hope to travel back to again soon.
